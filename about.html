GREEN INITIATIVE

Every day we strive to make our facilities more sustainable and eco-friendly. Our commitment to environmental protection includes a recycling program, low-flow faucets, cooking oil recycling, using biodegradable containers and paper straws, washing towels upon request, and installing key card-based light controls.
